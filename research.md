<section class="research-section">
  <h2>Active Projects</h2>

  <article class="research-project">
    <h3>NASA ULI: AAM Demand Modeling</h3>
    <p>
      Developing demand forecasting pipelines for mobility-energy coordinated AAM systems using
      data-driven and neurosymbolic approaches.
    </p>
    <ul>
      <li>Rolling-horizon trip request generation for scalable simulation.</li>
      <li>Temporal demand estimation for regional and urban scenarios.</li>
      <li>Decision support for infrastructure and policy planning.</li>
    </ul>
  </article>

  <article class="research-project">
    <h3>Neurosymbolic Methods for Transportation AI</h3>
    <p>
      Designing interpretable prediction models by combining symbolic structure with neural learning.
    </p>
    <ul>
      <li>Knowledge-guided model design and rule-aware prediction.</li>
      <li>Symbolic distillation and reasoning-integrated workflows.</li>
      <li>Applications in demand prediction and planning support.</li>
    </ul>
  </article>

  <article class="research-project">
    <h3>AI-Assisted Disaster Response Planning</h3>
    <p>
      Building optimization-driven frameworks to improve mobility response under emergency constraints.
    </p>
    <ul>
      <li>Neural-accelerated genetic optimization strategies.</li>
      <li>Multi-objective scheduling and constrained resource planning.</li>
      <li>Scenario-driven analysis for resilient operations.</li>
    </ul>
  </article>
</section>
